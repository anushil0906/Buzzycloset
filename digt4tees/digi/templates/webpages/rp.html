Cancellation And Refund And Return

7-DAY RETURNS Items purchased from BUZZYCLOSET are eligible for return/exchange, if returned within 7 days of delivery.

You can return the product if you don't like the fabric, color, fit or for any reason - We don't have a problem. However, Rs. 100 will be deducted as Returns handling charges. This is something that is charged to us by the logistic partners. Items must be returned unused, unwashed, unworn with all original tags attached. Make sure you put the item(s) back in the original package along with the invoice in the same way it was delivered.

In case of size issues, your product will be eligible for exchange/replacement with the preferential size. Two-way courier charges (return handling charges + replaced product delivery charges) will be applied during the exchange process. For Refund one way charges will be applied.

We accept all cancellations made before the cut-off time of 1 day. Please reach out to our customer services for cancellation requests and refunds. We refund all payments made for orders placed as a store credit, which can be used by customers for future purchases. If we suspect any fraudulent transaction or transactions violating the terms of website use, we are at sole discretion to cancel such orders.
